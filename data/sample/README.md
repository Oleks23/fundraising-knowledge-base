# FCC Sample Data

This folder contains sample FCC-owned CSV records for the Microsoft 365 MVP scenarios:

- Annual Giving / Spring Appeal
- Major Gifts / Principal Gifts Portfolio

The sample data is intentionally an operational overlay, not a copy of source-system activity records.

## Activity Handling

No FCC-owned activity records are included.

Relationship activities remain in RE NXT Actions or equivalent CRM task/activity records. When a sample commitment or risk references `RE NXT Actions`, the action is source data only. FCC owns the resulting commitment, risk, or metric snapshot, not the activity itself.

Operational activities remain in Planner or other planning tools. When a sample commitment or risk references `Planner`, the record represents an FCC-managed operational obligation or risk derived from Planner source data. It is not a replica of a Planner task.

## Readiness And Compliance

Sprint 1 sample metric snapshots avoid unapproved readiness scoring formulas. Metrics are simple counts or clearly marked placeholders such as `commitment_compliance_placeholder` and `follow_up_compliance_placeholder`.
